Nature is the physical world surrounding us, encompassing everything from vast oceans and towering mountains to air, soil, and wildlife. It is our primary life support system, providing essential resources like oxygen, water, and food while offering immense, calming beauty. Protecting nature is crucial for sustaining life, as it maintains ecological balance and provides a sanctuary for both mental well-being and biodiversity. 

    Nature encompasses all living
        (plants, animals, microorganisms) and non-living (rocks, water, atmosphere)
        components of the Earth that exist without human intervention.
